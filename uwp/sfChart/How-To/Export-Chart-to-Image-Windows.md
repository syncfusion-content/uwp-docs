### Export Chart to Image (Windows 8.1)

The export chart to image feature in the SfChart control enables you to export the image of the chart in different image file formats. 

Supported Formats

* JPG or JPEG
* JPG-XR
* GIF
* TIFF
* PNG
* BMP
